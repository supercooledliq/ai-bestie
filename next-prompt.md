# Enhanced Stitch Prompt: Login/Signup Screen

A highly polished, empowering, and modern login/signup screen for the AI Bestie app. It features a soft, glassmorphic aesthetic with vibrant accents, feeling personal, inviting, and premium.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Mobile-first
- Theme: Light, modern glassmorphism, warm, airy
- Background: Soft Pearl / Warm Off-White (#F8F6F7)
- Surface: Glass White (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)) for the main frosted glass auth card
- Primary Accent: Vibrant Magenta-Pink (#E8307D) for primary actions, buttons, and glowing accents
- Secondary Accent: Whisper Pink (#FCEEF3) for solid input backgrounds
- Text Primary: Deep Maroon-Black (#1A0F14) for headings
- Typography: Plus Jakarta Sans, heavy font weights for headers, relaxed spacing for body copy
- Component Style: Pill-shaped buttons (rounded-full), dramatically rounded cards (rounded-3xl) with subtle, warm drop-shadows (soft glow effect).
- Layout: Generous whitespace, floating cards with side padding.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header Image Area:** Top 40% of the screen. A bright, empowering, abstract soft-pink background image or gradient with a motivational headline ("You are your own muse.") and subtext.
2. **Glassmorphic Auth Card:** A gently floating card overlapping the bottom of the header image. It uses `backdrop-blur-xl` and contains the form elements.
3. **Toggle Switch:** A pill-shaped segmented control at the top of the card to switch between "Login" and "Sign Up".
4. **Form Fields:** Borderless input fields (Email, Password) on a soft Whisper Pink (#FCEEF3) background with heavily rounded corners. Include a visibility toggle icon in the password field.
5. **Primary Button:** A highly visible, pill-shaped Vibrant Magenta-Pink (#E8307D) "Continue" button with a warm, soft drop-shadow.
6. **Divider:** A subtle "or connect with" horizontal divider.
7. **Social Logins:** A 2-column grid of pill-shaped buttons for Google (light) and Apple (dark).
8. **Footer Link:** A centered text link at the very bottom of the card for "Forgot your password?".
